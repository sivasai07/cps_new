Frequently Asked Questions

How does LearnPath personalize my learning?

Our AI assesses your initial knowledge, learning style, and goals through a series of questions and adaptive tests. It then continuously adjusts your path based on your progress and performance.

What kind of courses are available?

LearnPath offers a wide range of courses across various domains including technology, arts, sciences, business, and personal development. We are constantly expanding our library.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, LearnPath is fully responsive and accessible on all devices, including smartphones and tablets, through your web browser. A dedicated mobile app is currently under development.
